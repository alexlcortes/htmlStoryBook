Chapter I

The cold wakes you from a deep sleep. The soreness from laying on a metal shelf runs across your body. You reach for a blanket that was never there as your numb fingers close around nothing but more cold. Your eyes adjust to the darkness of the small coffin the crew calls a room. Then you notice a flashing warning light above the room door. The red alert is silent. You instantly listen for other sounds coming from the station but all you can hear is the absence of sounds.
